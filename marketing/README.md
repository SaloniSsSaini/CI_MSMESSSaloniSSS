# CI_MSME Marketing Data

This folder contains Excel spreadsheets with comprehensive marketing contact data for Carbon Intelligence MSME platform.

## Files Created

### 1. MSME_Contacts_Marketing.xlsx
Contains detailed contact information for MSMEs (Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises) with the following sheets:

- **MSME_Contacts**: Main contact sheet with 15 MSMEs including:
  - Company Name, Contact Person, Email, Phone
  - Industry, Business Domain, Company Type
  - Location (City, State)
  - Business metrics (Annual Turnover, Employees)
  - Carbon Score and Registration details
  - Status (Active/Inactive)

- **Summary**: Key metrics and statistics
- **Industry_Breakdown**: Analysis by industry sector
- **State_Breakdown**: Geographic distribution analysis

### 2. Banks_NBFCs_Marketing.xlsx
Contains contact information for financial institutions offering green finance solutions:

- **All_Institutions**: Complete list of 16 institutions (8 Banks + 8 NBFCs)
- **Banks_Only**: Public and Private sector banks only
- **NBFCs_Only**: Non-Banking Financial Companies only
- **Summary**: Key metrics and statistics
- **Type_Breakdown**: Analysis by institution type
- **City_Breakdown**: Geographic distribution analysis

## Data Sources

The data includes:
- **Banks**: State Bank of India, HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Axis Bank, Kotak Mahindra Bank, Bank of Baroda, Canara Bank, Punjab National Bank
- **NBFCs**: Tata Capital, Bajaj Finance, Mahindra Finance, L&T Finance, Aditya Birla Finance, Shriram Finance, Cholamandalam Finance, Muthoot Finance
- **MSMEs**: Diverse range of companies across manufacturing, services, agriculture, and other sectors
