# Green Finance Market Analysis - India (2024-2030)

## Executive Summary

Based on comprehensive analysis of the Indian green finance market, this report provides insights into market size, growth trends, key drivers, and opportunities. The green finance market in India is experiencing rapid growth driven by regulatory support, increasing ESG compliance requirements, and growing awareness of sustainable finance among MSMEs.
