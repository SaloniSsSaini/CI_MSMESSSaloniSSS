## Technology Assessment

### Available Technologies

#### 1. Ultrasonic Mist Generators
- **Water Recycling Potential**: High (90-95%)
- **Best Applications**: Indoor humidification, precision agriculture
- **Advantages**: Precise control, low energy consumption
- **Challenges**: Mineral buildup, bacterial growth

#### 2. Compressed Air Systems
- **Water Recycling Potential**: Medium (60-80%)
- **Best Applications**: Industrial cooling, dust suppression
- **Advantages**: High output, works with various water qualities
- **Challenges**: Aerosol contamination, scale formation

#### 3. High-Pressure Nozzle Systems
- **Water Recycling Potential**: High (85-95%)
- **Best Applications**: Outdoor cooling, greenhouse systems
- **Advantages**: Large coverage area, durable
- **Challenges**: Water quality requirements, maintenance

### Water Treatment Technologies

#### Filtration Systems
- **Mechanical Filtration**: Remove suspended solids
- **Activated Carbon**: Remove organic contaminants
- **Reverse Osmosis**: Remove dissolved minerals
- **UV Sterilization**: Eliminate biological contaminants

#### Treatment Processes
- **Coagulation/Flocculation**: Remove fine particles
- **Sedimentation**: Separate solids from water
- **Disinfection**: Chemical or UV treatment
- **pH Adjustment**: Optimize water chemistry
